About TMWS

A welcoming space for Malayalee culture, connection, and community service in Tambaram.

Who we are

TMWS, along with CTMA FAIMA Ayalkootam , connects Malayalee families in Tambaram and surrounding areas. We celebrate traditions, organize events, and build strong bonds among families through cultural and social initiatives.

What we do

Onam & Vishu programs, family meetups, kids competitions, and member support—planned to encourage participation across all ages.
