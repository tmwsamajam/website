About TMWS

A welcoming space for Malayalee culture, connection, and community service in Tambaram.

Who we are

Tambaram Malayalee Welfare Samajam (TMWS) connects Malayalees in Tambaram and surrounding areas. We celebrate traditions, organize events, and build strong bonds among families through cultural and social initiatives.

What we do

Onam & Vishu programs, family meetups, kids competitions, and member support—planned to encourage participation across all ages.
