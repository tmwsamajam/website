About Us

A welcoming space for Malayalee culture, connection, and community service in Tambaram.

Tambaram Malayalee Welfare Samajam (TMWS) is a vibrant community connecting Malayalees in Tambaram and surrounding areas.


We celebrate traditions, organize events, and build strong bonds among families through cultural and social initiatives.

Our Activities

Celebrations, learning, and togetherness—planned with families in mind.

Onam & Vishu Celebrations

Traditional programs, cultural performances, and community meals.

Family Get-togethers

Friendly meetups that strengthen connections across families.

Kids Competitions

Fun events that encourage confidence, creativity, and participation.
