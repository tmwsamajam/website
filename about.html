About TMWS

TMWS, along with CTMA FAIMA Ayalkootam , connects Malayalee families in Tambaram and surrounding areas. We celebrate traditions, organize events, and build strong bonds among families through cultural and social initiatives.

Mission

Strengthen the Malayalee community in Tambaram through inclusive cultural programs, family activities, and social support initiatives.

Vision

Celebrate Kerala’s heritage while nurturing a welcoming space for new friendships, learning, and community service.

Social & Cultural Activities

Programs that bring together families, youth, and elders—throughout the year.

Festivals

Onam, Vishu, and other community celebrations with cultural performances.

Arts & Learning

Inspired by initiatives that promote language, education, and cultural development, we encourage children and youth to actively learn, participate, and showcase their talents through various programs and activities. Our efforts focus on preserving cultural identity while fostering knowledge, creativity, and community engagement. Learn more at Kerala Govt Initiative .
